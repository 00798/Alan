About Career

Alan Walker was born in Northampton, England and moved to Bergen, Norway when he was two years old. Growing up, he was heavily influenced by video games and films from which he drew inspiration for his music career. In 2012, he started creating EDM tracks under the name DJ Walkzz and eventually changed it to Alan Walker. In 2014 he released his first single Fade, which gained traction in the music scene when it was featured in a YouTube video by YouTuber “PewDiePie” which helped boost its popularity. His single Faded was released in December 2015 and quickly became an international hit single, topping the charts in 10 countries. His other singles include Alone, Sing Me To Sleep, and All Falls Down. In addition to his solo work, he has collaborated with several other artists such as Au/Ra, K-391, and Juliander. Alan Walker’s ImpactAlan Walker has had a significant impact on the EDM scene as well as the music industry as a whole. His success with Faded has allowed him to collaborate with other artists and reach a wider audience. He has also been featured on several soundtracks for movies and television shows such as Ready Player One and The 100. He has become an advocate for mental health awareness and has partnered with UNICEF to help raise funds for children in need. In 2020, he was named one of Forbes’ “30 Under 30” list of most influential people in Europe within the entertainment industry.
